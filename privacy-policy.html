Blind Street India

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 18, 2026

Overview

Blind Street India ("the App") is an accessibility-awareness mobile game developed by Vishesh Nagpal. This privacy policy explains how the App handles information.

✦ This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Crash reports sent to external servers
  • Financial or payment information

How the App Works

Blind Street India is an offline game. All gameplay data (level progress, confidence scores) exists only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

On first launch, the App downloads 3D model files from public GitHub release assets. These are read-only game assets — no user data is sent during this process.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with:

  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics platforms (Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • Social media SDKs
  • Payment processors

The only network request the App makes is downloading game model files from GitHub on first launch. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to that connection.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since we collect no data at all, there is no risk to children's privacy.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL. The effective date at the top of the page will reflect the most recent update.
